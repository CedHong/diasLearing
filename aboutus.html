ABOUT US

The ongoing Covid-19 pandemic has caused school closures across the globe, making many in-class lessons impossible. Although children are forced to stay at home during this time, they need continued access to engaging educational content.

Finding relevant French materials for children can be difficult. Diaslearning.com serves as an educational platform to make learning French more accessible. This free resource intends to provide support for parents and students seeking additional learning.

All lessons on this website are original works designed by certified teachers according to the Ontarian curriculum. This resource is currently unaffiliated with any school board or any ministry of education.
