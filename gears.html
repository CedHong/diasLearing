Les Engrenages

(Gears)

Lesson Plan for Teachers and Parents:

(coming soon)

Recommended Level:

Grade 4 Science

Learning Goals:
  1. Identify and describe different types of gears
  2. Describe the purposes of various gear systems
Curriculum Connections For Parents & Teachers:
  1. Describe the purposes of gear systems (e.g., to facilitate changes in direction, speed, or force)
  2. Describe, using their observations, how gears operate in one plane (e.g., spur gears, idler gears) and in two planes (e.g., crown, bevel, or worm gears)
  3. Explain how the gear system on a bicycle works

Il existe plusieurs formes sortes d'engrenages dont les 4 formes suivantes:

  • • Les engrenages droits (Spur gears)
  • • Les engrenages coniques (Bevel gears)
  • • Les engrenages composés (Composite gears)
  • • Les engrenages enchaînés (Sprocket gear)
